1. Overview

Dabis Content Manager ("the App") is a local desktop application designed to help you manage and upload videos to YouTube. The App operates entirely on your device. When you use YouTube features, the App communicates directly with Google/YouTube Services from your device. We do not operate servers that collect, store, or process your content or Google account data.

2. Google User Data: What We Access

When you authorize the App via Google OAuth, the App may access the following types of Google user data depending on the permissions ("scopes") you grant:

  • YouTube Channel Information: Your YouTube channel name, channel ID, and basic channel metadata to identify which channel you are managing.
  • YouTube Video Management: The ability to upload videos, update video metadata (titles, descriptions, tags, thumbnails, privacy settings), manage playlists, and schedule video publications on your behalf.
  • OAuth Tokens: Access tokens and refresh tokens provided by Google to maintain your authenticated session.

The App requests only the minimum scopes necessary to provide its core video upload and management functionality.

3. Google User Data: How We Use It

We use the Google user data described above solely for the following purposes:

  • Video Uploads: To upload video files from your device to your YouTube channel.
  • Metadata Management: To set and update video titles, descriptions, tags, thumbnails, privacy settings, and scheduling options.
  • Playlist Management: To add videos to playlists on your YouTube channel.
  • Session Maintenance: To keep you signed in so you do not need to re-authorize on every use.

We do not use your Google user data for:

  • Advertising, marketing, or promotional purposes
  • Selling or renting data to third parties
  • Building user profiles for purposes unrelated to the App's functionality
  • Any purpose other than providing and improving the App's user-facing video management features

4. Google User Data: Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your Google user data to any third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Google/YouTube Services: When you initiate an action (such as uploading a video or updating metadata), the App sends your data directly to Google/YouTube APIs. This is necessary to perform the requested function. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, but only to the extent legally required.

We do not:

  • Transfer Google user data to any advertising services
  • Transfer Google user data to data brokers
  • Transfer Google user data to information resellers
  • Use Google user data for any purpose other than providing the App's core functionality

5. Google User Data: Storage and Protection

All Google user data is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit or store your Google user data on any external servers.

What is stored locally:

  • OAuth Tokens: Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored in a local configuration file on your device to maintain your authenticated session.
  • Channel Information: Basic channel identifiers may be cached locally to improve the user experience.
  • No Video Storage: Your video files remain on your device; we do not copy or store them elsewhere.

Security measures:

  • OAuth tokens are stored using standard local file storage mechanisms provided by the operating system.
  • The App does not transmit tokens or credentials to any server other than Google's official OAuth endpoints.
  • You are responsible for securing your device (e.g., using device encryption, strong passwords, and keeping your operating system updated).

6. Google User Data: Retention and Deletion

Data Retention:

  • Google user data (OAuth tokens, cached channel information) remains stored locally on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
  • We do not retain any Google user data on external servers because we do not operate such servers.

How to Delete Your Data:

  • Within the App: Use the App's sign-out or disconnect feature to remove stored OAuth tokens and cached data.
  • Manual Deletion: Delete the App's configuration folder from your device (typically located in your user profile's application data directory).
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App will remove locally stored data.
  • Revoke Access via Google: Visit your Google Account Permissions page to revoke the App's access to your Google account. This immediately invalidates all OAuth tokens.

Data Deletion Requests: Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over deletion. If you have sent us any data directly (e.g., support emails), you may request deletion by contacting us at dabinuss@gmail.com.
